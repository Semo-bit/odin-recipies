Chicken Skewer

Description

This is a recipe for easy chicken skewers made form ground chicken and simple ingredients

Takes a few minutes to cook and prep and is delicious to serve with a side of rice or veggies

Ingredients

  • A pound of ground chicken
  • Half a cup of bread crumbs
  • 1 tbsp of garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl add all the ingredients
  2. Mix all ingredients until all the spices and bread crumbs are homogenous with the chicken
  3. Wrap the chicken mixture onto a skewers (should make around 10 skewers)
  4. Turn on the stove to a medium high heat
  5. Once the pan is hot add the skewers a few at a time keeping space in between them
  6. Cook the skewer of medium high heat for about 5 mins
  7. Once the side is done turn the skewers and cook for another 5 mins
  8. Take the skewers off the heat and serve, either over rice or next to a salad
